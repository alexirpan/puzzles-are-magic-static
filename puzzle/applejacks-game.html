Applejack's Game

View Solution


View Stats

The Mane 6 take a look around. "Isn't there supposed to be a puzzle here?"

Applejack looks around. "Well, I don't see one. Maybe it'll show up if we wait a bit. Hey, why don't we play a game to pass the time?"

"Really, Applejack?"

"I promise it'll be quick," Applejack says, as she starts shuffling two decks of red and green cards. "Hang on, are we missing a few cards?"

Applejack's Game

"The rules are simple," Applejack says. "We each take turn judging cards. I deal out a green card, and everypony else submits a red card. I pick the winner, and the winner becomes the new judge for next round."
