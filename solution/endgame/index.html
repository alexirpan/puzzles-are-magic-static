Solution - Endgame

By Alex Irpan

Upon opening the puzzle, solvers are given a word search, and a list of 36 blanks, grouped into six groups of 6. Each group is labeled with an Element of Harmony, after their shapes change to match the Mane 6's cutie marks. However, their colors are different. For example, the Element of Loyalty is the lightning bolt, but should be red, not yellow.

It turns out this puzzle is about a different set of six magical artifacts that are stronger when wielded together: the Infinity Stones! In the note given, T is Thanos, who was drawn to Equestria in his search for the Infinity Stones.

Each word in the word search can have one of the Infinity Stone names added to the start or end, to form a new phrase that fits into the given blanks. As an additional hint to the Marvel connection and puzzle mechanic, when all 36 words are found, the leftover letters in the word search spell out "YOU HAVE FOUND EVERY WORD MARVELOUS JOB NOW ADD A WORD TO EACH".

Authors Notes

During hunt design, I knew I wanted the story to revisit the series premiere, and the endgame puzzle should be tied to the Elements of Harmony, but for a while, I didn't know what puzzle would fit. After a lot of time, I had this idle thought.

"Hey, isn't it funny how there are six Elements of Harmony, and six Infinity Stones, and these are both magical artifacts that are stronger together? Wait a second...OH MY GOD I HAVE TO MAKE THIS PUZZLE."

I considered using more Marvel references, as well as other ways of giving the words, but eventually decided a word search was the easiest way to give the base words without directly giving away the Infinity Stone mechanic. Besides, the hunt already had a crossword puzzle and cryptics puzzle. I also liked that it made the puzzle itself pretty easy. My least favorite puzzlehunt experiences are when my team gets stuck on an endgame puzzle, so I wanted to keep them straightforward.

Sorry about some of the weirder phrases (SoulPancake, Reality Bites). To reduce noise in the word search, I used longer words when possible, and those were the best options I found in my wordlist.

One of the reality clues used to be the Reality Gauntlet from Danny Phantom, which was inspired by the Infinity Gauntlet. I thought this was yet another cute Marvel connection, but when a testsolver found the NSA leaker Reality Winner, I switched the clue to that. Reality Winner is the best name. I had to use it.
