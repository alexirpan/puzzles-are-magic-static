Solution - Cross Eyed

By Alex Irpan, Anderson Wang, and Nishant Pappireddi

This is a standard crossword, except in every clue, the letters within each word are scrambled. The first and last letter of each word are unchanged, based on the classic myth that you can jumble the middle letters of a word without hurting readability. As you may have discovered, this myth is only partly true. Longer words with uncommon letter patterns take more effort to read.

The crossword fill works the same way. Each word must be entered such that the first and last letter are in their original spots, but the middle letters may appear in any order.
