Hint to start puzzle: Most of these clues seem to be nonsense, but there are a few that have somewhat normal answers, such as "Perfect society (6)" and "Large visual organ (3 3)." Try Googling a few of the answers together to see what they have in common.
Hint for extraction: The stars at the bottom of the page are a double clue: they indicate how you should extract from the Numbers resulting from each equation, and the number of stars is the length of the final answer.
